# Gel Nails - Learning Notes

**These are my personal learning notes. They are not expert guidance.**

---

I picked up gel nails as a hobby and quickly realized I didn't understand what I was actually putting on my nails or how any of it worked. I wanted to enjoy this hobby safely so I decided to learn the chemistry behind it. This repo is a living document of that learning process, I will update as I come across new information and as time allows.

I am a hobbyist. I have a general interest in understanding things properly, but I am not a chemist, a dermatologist, or a nail professional. I've tried to be accurate, and I've linked to the sources I used, but please don't treat my notes as gospel. Treat them as a starting point and a map to the real sources. The links in each file are where the actual knowledge lives. I'd encourage you to read at least some of them yourself, especially anything in the safety section. The risks are real and worth understanding in your own words, not just mine.

If something I've written conflicts with what a source actually says, trust the source.
